Super Kitty Wordle

Tutorial: The goal of this game is to guess a word from Roaming Kitty blogs. The word can be of any length. To submit a guess, enter your guess, username and password. Then click the Submit button. The game will display coloring of your submission. A grey background means this alphabet is not in the word. A yellow background means the alphabet is in the word but at the wrong position. A green background means the alphabet is in the word and at the right position.
